Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 16, 2026

Marimonda ("the Game") is a free iOS game developed by Boli Games. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the Game collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

Game Center

The Game integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is operated entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center data beyond what Apple makes available through the standard Game Center APIs (e.g., your player alias and scores).

Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact
