Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Tap anywhere on the screen to make the marimonda fly. Avoid the Congos and try to score as many points as you can!

How do I see leaderboards and achievements?

Marimonda uses Apple Game Center. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device's Settings. Then tap Options from the main menu to view leaderboards.

How do I turn off sound?

Tap Options from the main menu and toggle the sound setting.

The app is crashing or not working correctly

Please make sure you're running the latest version of iOS. If the problem persists, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll look into it.

Is the game free?

Yes! Marimonda is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.
